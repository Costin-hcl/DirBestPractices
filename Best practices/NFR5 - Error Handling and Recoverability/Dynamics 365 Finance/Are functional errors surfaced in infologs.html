Surfacing functional errors to business users

Application-level errors stemming from business rule validations should be surfaced to business users as they are the ones who can correct the data. Examples include:

  • journals not balanced
  • correcting packing slip below quantity matched by invoice

Do not "swallow" errors - either show them in an infolog if they are relevant for business users or log them to Application Insights if they're technical (e.g. call stacks, HTTP errors).
